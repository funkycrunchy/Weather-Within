# Weather Within Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 June 2026

Weather Within is a private self-help and wellbeing app for emotional check-ins, coping tools, reflection, weekly questionnaire tracking, and support information.

This privacy policy explains what information the app stores, how it is used, and what control you have over it.

Weather Within is not a medical app or medical device. It does not provide diagnosis, treatment, clinical monitoring, emergency care, medication advice, therapy, or professional mental-health support.

## Summary

Weather Within is designed to be private by default.

In the current version:

- You do not need an account.
- The app does not sell your data.
- The app does not use advertising trackers.
- The app does not cloud sync your entries.
- The app stores your entries locally on your device.
- The app does not send your check-ins, thought records, questionnaire answers, or coping-tool entries to a server.

## Information Stored By The App

Weather Within may store the following information locally on your device:

- Preferred name, if you choose to add one.
- Inner weather check-ins.
- Generated forecasts.
- Body signals and support needs selected during check-ins.
- Future-me notes.
- CBT-style thought records.
- Storm Plan entries.
- Coping-tool and wellness-tool entries.
- Tool feedback and local learning evidence.
- Weekly GAD-7 questionnaire answers and scores.
- Weekly PHQ-9 questionnaire answers and scores.
- Support-region preference, such as UK or US.
- Onboarding and settings preferences.
- Reminder preference for weekly questionnaire notifications.
- Biometric/device-lock preference.

Some of this information may be sensitive because it relates to mental health, mood, symptoms, coping strategies, or personal safety planning.

## How This Information Is Used

Weather Within uses locally stored information to provide app features, including:

- Showing your latest forecast.
- Saving your check-in history.
- Showing mood, anxiety, and depression trends.
- Reopening previous thought records.
- Showing your Storm Plan.
- Remembering your support-region setting.
- Reminding you when weekly questionnaires are due, if you enable reminders.
- Learning which coping tools appear useful for similar moments.
- Recommending tools based on your check-ins, tool use, questionnaire context, and saved feedback.

The app's learning system is local. It is intended to make suggestions feel more relevant without sending your personal entries away from your device.

## Data Sharing

Weather Within does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

In the current version, Weather Within does not transmit your app entries to the developer, to cloud storage, or to third-party analytics services.

The app may open external phone, SMS, or web apps when you choose to contact support services or open support links. Those external services are separate from Weather Within and have their own privacy practices.

If you use the Export local data feature, the app creates a plain-text JSON copy of your local app data and opens your device's share options. You choose where that exported file or text goes. Once exported, that copy is outside Weather Within's control.

## Permissions

Weather Within may request these permissions:

### Notifications

Used only if you enable weekly questionnaire reminders. Notifications are created locally on your device.

### Biometric Or Device Credential Unlock

Used only if you enable the app lock. Weather Within does not receive or store your fingerprint, face data, passcode, or biometric template. Authentication is handled by Android.

### Phone, SMS, Or Browser Actions

The app may open your phone, SMS app, or browser when you choose a support option. Weather Within does not place calls or send messages without you taking action in the relevant app.

## Data Retention

Weather Within keeps local data until you delete it, uninstall the app, clear the app's storage, or use the Erase local data option.

Because the current version stores data locally on your device, the developer cannot remotely access, recover, correct, or delete your entries.

## Your Choices And Control

You can:

- Use the app without creating an account.
- Avoid adding a preferred name.
- Choose whether to enable questionnaire reminders.
- Choose whether to enable biometric/device lock.
- Change the support region in Settings.
- Export local data from Settings.
- Erase local app data from Settings.
- Delete app data through Android system settings.
- Uninstall the app to remove its local app storage from the device.

## Security

Weather Within uses Android app-private storage for local data.

You can optionally enable biometric or device-credential unlock for an extra app-opening prompt.

No app can guarantee complete security. If you share your device, export your data, back up your phone, or allow another person to access your unlocked device, they may be able to access information stored in or exported from the app.

Before a wider public release, the app may move sensitive structured data to a stronger encrypted storage layer.

## Children

Weather Within is not designed for children.

If the app is made available publicly, the intended age range and any child-safety requirements should be reviewed before release.

## Wellbeing And Safety Information

Weather Within provides self-help and tracking tools only.

GAD-7 and PHQ-9 results are for personal wellbeing tracking and optional discussion with a qualified professional. They are not a diagnosis, treatment recommendation, clinical assessment, or medical advice.

If you may hurt yourself or someone else, or life is in immediate danger, contact emergency services now.

The app cannot determine whether you are safe.

## Changes To This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated as Weather Within changes.

If future versions add accounts, cloud sync, analytics, crash reporting, online AI features, or other data sharing, this policy should be updated before those features are released.
